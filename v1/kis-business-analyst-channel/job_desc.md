# KIS
**Role:** Business Analyst (Channel)

---

Mô tả công việc
• Receive requests, collect information, and analyze business requirements to clarify product expectations and objectives.

• Analyze and propose appropriate models and solutions based on the collected requirements.

• Prepare business requirement specification (BRS) documents, use case specifications, and design wireframes or prototypes.

• Provide business-oriented consultation based on thorough analysis and research.

• Collaborate closely with the Development team throughout the software development process to ensure all members fully understand the business requirements.

• Create and maintain system documentation and user manuals; organize user training sessions and provide support to resolve issues during system usage.

• Perform other tasks as assigned by direct managers or leadership.

Yêu cầu công việc
Mức độ phù hợp và xếp hạng của bạn so với ứng viên khác như thế nào?
Requirements:

• Bachelor's degree in Information Technology, Information Systems, Finance, Economics, Banking, or related fields.

• At least 3 years working experience in BA, exposure to financial services or securities is a plus.

• Preference will be given to candidates with (internship) experience in business analysis or product development for companies in Fintech, Digital Banking, E-payment, or Securities.

• Proficient in Microsoft Office (Word, Excel, PowerPoint) and familiar with BA tools such as Visio, Axure, Balsamiq, Figma.

• Passionate about exploring product trends in the financial industry.

• Strong analytical, documentation, and communication skills.

• Ability to work both independently and collaboratively in a team environment.

• Annual bonus/awards for outstanding employees.

• Direct training and mentorship from experienced industry professionals.

• Opportunities to participate in workshops and professional events to enhance knowledge and stay updated on market trends.

• Expand your professional network and develop a long-term career in the securities industry.

• Annual health check-ups, company trips, and team-building activities.
